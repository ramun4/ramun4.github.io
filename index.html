Ramunė Staškevičiūtė

I am primarily a painter, but will occasionally find myself doing watercolor, printmaking as well as photography work. In my work, I have never adhered to any fashion trends. I do what I want and only add to my work what I considered necessary. I am residing in Lithuanian together with my beloved husband and my adorable dogs.


Exhibitions & Awards
  • Organized 14 personal exhibitions in Lithuania
  • Participated in 43 group exhibitions in Lithuania
  • Diploma in the exhibition “ Stamp”, 2002
  • Winner of the large format work competition in 2004 at Meno Parkas Gallery, Kaunas, Lithuania
  • LAA's prize for the “Indulgences” in the exhibition “The Best Work of 2016” at Kaunas Picture Gallery, Lithuania
  • Award for the “Abandoned Territories” in the exhibition “The Best Work of 2018” at Kaunas Picture Gallery, Lithuania
